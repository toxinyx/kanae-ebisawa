Another long day, nothing much was different. By the time I woke up, mama was already gone. Pa didn’t even come home. They work so hard, all the time; I rarely see them, to no one’s surprise. Sometimes I… just really wish we could sit down and talk. There’s just never any time.

It’s always left to me to keep the house in check and look after little Kenta. Not that I’m complaining, of course, I adore him, but… when was the last time I really had time for me? He followed me everywhere today. Kept tugging on my sleeve and asking why the beams of our house creak when the wind blows. I told him that it’s because the wood is alive- that it breathes with us. He… liked that answer, I think. At least he seemed to.

After he finished breakfast, we went outside to tighten the ropes by the shed. My hands are still stinging from having to tighten them so much, but if I don't, the rain will push the wood loose; I really need pa to get round to fixing that. I might end up having to do it myself, I guess. Kenta tried to help, but the hammer was too heavy for him. He’d giggle everytime he dropped it; I laughed too, but part of me was worried he’d end up smashing his foot.

By the time the sun was at the peak, I was already exhausted. Not much to be done, considering there was still wood to cut and a floor to sweep.

– Kanae <3
